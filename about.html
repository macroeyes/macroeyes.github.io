ABOUT

macroeyes™ is a visual search platform for data

macroeyes makes it possible to search – and understand – connections across vast realms of data.

 

In a globalized business environment, data only becomes actionable when viewed in a larger context.

CEO Benjamin Fels led market and quant teams for a private fund in Chicago and London. He graduated Wesleyan with high honors in art history and philosophy; his thesis work in pattern recognition was the basis of successful derivatives trading strategies.

 

Chief Scientist Suvrit Sra, PhD is a machine learning and large-scale data analysis expert. He is Sr. Research Scientist at Max-Planck Institute for Intelligent Systems and a visiting professor at Carnegie Mellon.

 

Chief Design Officers Sebastian Kaupert and Theo Fels have directed critical design initiatives across platforms and industries. They've designed a virtual corporate HQ for American Express, built Bloomberg.com, MSNBC.com and recently led the redesign of the digital presence of the IMF.
